Datapixx(‘SetPropixx3DCrosstalkRL’)

[[Psychtoolbox]] › [[Datapixx]].{mex*,dll} subfunction

Set observed 3D left <- right-eye crosstalk (range 0-1). The PROPixx will
subtract this amount of the right-eye image from the left-eye image. This should
eliminate most left-eye crosstalk in 3D applications.
