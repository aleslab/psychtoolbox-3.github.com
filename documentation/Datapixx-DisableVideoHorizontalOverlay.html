Datapixx(‘DisableVideoHorizontalOverlay’)

[[Psychtoolbox]] › [[Datapixx]].{mex*,dll} subfunction

Disables blending of left and right halves of the video frame. See
EnableVideoHorizontalOverlay for more information, and
DatapixxGazeContingentDemo.m for an example.
