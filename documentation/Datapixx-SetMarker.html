Datapixx(‘SetMarker’)

[[Psychtoolbox]] › [[Datapixx]].{mex*,dll} subfunction

Latch the current Datapixx time value into the marker register. This could be
followed by RegWrVideoSync or RegWrPixelSync to latch a video stimulus onset
timetag with microsecond precision.
