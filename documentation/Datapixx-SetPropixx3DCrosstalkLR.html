Datapixx(‘SetPropixx3DCrosstalkLR’)

[[Psychtoolbox]] › [[Datapixx]].{mex*,dll} subfunction

Set observed 3D left -> right-eye crosstalk (range 0-1). The PROPixx will
subtract this amount of the left-eye image from the right-eye image. This should
eliminate most right-eye crosstalk in 3D applications.
