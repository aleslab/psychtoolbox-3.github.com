PsychOculusVRCore(‘EndFrameTiming’)

[[Psychtoolbox]] › [[PsychOculusVRCore]].{mex*,dll} subfunction

Mark end of a new 3D head tracked render and presentation cycle for Oculus
device ‘oculusPtr’.
You usually won’t call this function yourself, but Screen(‘Flip’) will call it
automatically for you at the appropriate moment.
