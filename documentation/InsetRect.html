[[Psychtoolbox]] › [[PsychRects]]

r = InsetRect(r,dh,dv)

Shrinks the rectangle by pushing in the left and right
sides, each by the amount dh, and the top and bottom by the amount dv.
Negative values of dh and dv produce expansion.
Also see PsychRects.
