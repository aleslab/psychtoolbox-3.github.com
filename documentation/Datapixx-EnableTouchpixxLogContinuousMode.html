Datapixx(‘EnableTouchpixxLogContinuousMode’)

[[Psychtoolbox]] › [[Datapixx]].{mex*,dll} subfunction

During a panel touch, continuous position/timetag updates will be logged at
200Hz.
See Touchpixx*Demo files for examples.
